AI Bulk Validation - Rulebook

This document precisely details every automated rule currently enforced by the AI validation system. If a product metadata payload fails any of these programmed checks, the AI automatically assigns a Rejected status.

1. Core Product Identification

Every product must contain basic identifying details for database sync.

  • GTIN Number: Cannot be blank.
    Example: 8901234567890
  • Brand Name: Cannot be blank.
  • Product Name: Cannot be blank.
  • Product Description: Cannot be blank.
  • Company Name: Cannot be blank.

2. Categorization & Taxation

The system strictly enforces category placement and tax codes for retail readiness.

  • Category: Cannot be blank.
    Example: Food, Electronics
  • Sub Category: Cannot be blank.
  • HS Code (Harmonized System): Cannot be blank.
  • IGST (Tax Rate): Cannot be blank.

3. Pricing & Market Information (MRP)

The product must contain at least one valid pricing tier.

  • MRP Value: Must be greater than zero. It cannot be 0, negative, or blank.
    Valid: 150.00 | Invalid: 0.00
  • Target Market: Cannot be blank.
  • MRP Location: Cannot be blank.

4. Lifecycle Dates

All dates must strictly follow the standard format: YYYY-MM-DD.

  • Activation Date: Must be a valid date.
    Valid: 2026-03-18 | Invalid: 18-03-2026
  • Deactivation Date: Must be a valid date.
  • MRP Activation Date: Must be a valid date.

5. Weights and Measurements (Net Content)

The Net Content format must strictly contain mathematical numbers. The system rejects text strings placed inside numeric fields.

  • Kilograms (kg) & Liters (l): Must be a raw number. Rule: Cannot exceed 3 digits in length.
    Valid: 1.5 | Invalid: 1000 (Should be 1 tonne)
  • Grams (g) & Milliliters (ml): Must be a raw number.
    Valid: 500 | Invalid: 500g (Text letters trigger math failure)

6. Food-Specific Regulations

These rules are ONLY enforced if the product's "Category" is explicitly set to Food.

  • FSSAI License Number: Must be exactly 14 digits and legally begin with the number 1.
    Valid: 12345678901234 | Invalid: 2345678901234
  • Food Type: Cannot be blank.
    Example: Vegetarian, Non-Vegetarian
  • Shelf Life Metrics: Value, Unit, and Based On cannot be blank.
    Example: 12, Months, From Date of Manufacture
Developer Note: Missing Exemption Bypass
The AI codebase currently does not contain an override rule for "Exemptions". If a product is legally granted an exemption for any of the fields above, the system will still forcefully reject it due to missing data. This requires a pending bypass update to the AI architecture.
